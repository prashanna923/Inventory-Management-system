About us

The Inventory Management System is a software application for the Windows platform. It allows businesses to manage their inventory levels, track inventory movements, and automate inventory tasks such as auto-reordering of products. The demand for inventory trackers in the business sector, is crucial in the current situation. The intention of the system is to simplify processes, boost efficiency, and minimize expenses. The application stores detailed information about the products, and their quantity including the sales.
